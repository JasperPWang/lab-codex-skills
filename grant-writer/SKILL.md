# Grant Writer

Use this for research proposal strategy and drafting.

Workflow:
1. Identify sponsor/program, call text, eligibility, review criteria, page limits, and deadline.
2. Extract the project's central problem, hypothesis, aims, innovation, approach, milestones, risks, team fit, and expected impact.
3. Draft in reviewer-facing structure: why now, why this team, why this approach, what can fail, and what evidence de-risks it.
4. Keep claims evidence-backed and aligned with the call.
5. Produce a compliance checklist before final delivery.

Do not invent preliminary results, collaborators, facilities, budget numbers, or sponsor requirements.
