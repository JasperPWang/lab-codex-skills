# Academic Research Skills Router

Prefer the existing global `academic-research-suite` for Codex-native work. When the user explicitly names a component, route to the installed component skill:

- `deep-research`: research question, literature review, systematic review, fact-check, meta-analysis.
- `academic-paper`: paper writing, outlines, abstracts, revision, citation checks, disclosure and rebuttal audit.
- `academic-paper-reviewer`: mock peer review and editorial decision.
- `academic-pipeline`: end-to-end research -> write -> review -> revise -> finalize workflow.

Keep human-in-the-loop checkpoints. Do not let the model invent contributions, citations, datasets, or evidence.
